## Wants
- Keep her younger brother Tovan away from council politics.
- Learn why strangers bow before they know her name.
- Decide whether a hidden crown is a rescue tool or a trap.

## Friction
Mara is brave in motion and frightened in stillness. She can outrun guards, debt collectors, and riots, but she cannot outrun a direct question from Orin.

## Voice Notes
Short sentences when scared. Jokes when cornered. She names streets by smell before she names them by district.

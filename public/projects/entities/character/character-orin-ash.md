## Wound
Orin betrayed the old queen by surviving. His every kindness has a ledger entry in his mind.

## Useful Skills
- Reads civic codes, dead languages, and forged ink by smell.
- Can fold a map so the true route appears only by candlelight.
- Knows which council clerks are afraid and which are ambitious.

## Breaking Point
If Mara refuses the crown, Orin must decide whether protecting her means protecting her choice.

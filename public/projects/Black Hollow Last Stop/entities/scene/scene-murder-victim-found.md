## Scene Goals
- Establish Rowan, the abandoned station, and the immediate contradiction.
- Put three clue categories in front of the player: body placement, footprints, drag marks.
- Let the first branch reveal player mindset: evidence-first or suspect-first.

## Player Choices
- Treat the station as a staged body dump.
- Question the former signalman first.

## Design Note
Do not solve the case here. The opening should make the player ask why the body was moved into this specific station.

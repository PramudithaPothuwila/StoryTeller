## Planning Notes
- Keep the station tactile: wet dust, old oil, sealed-up quiet, rusted signal boxes, rain in broken roof glass.
- The body placement should look meaningful before the player knows what it means.
- Platform 7, the signal room, and the service road culvert are the first explorable sub-areas.

## Investigation Function
- Public scene: body found inside the station.
- Physical contradiction: drag marks enter the station.
- Hidden motive: the killer needed the station's old records.

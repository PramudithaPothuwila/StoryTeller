## Sensory Palette
Blue-white glare, wet stone, citrus peels, brass bells, laundry snapping above canals.

## Story Use
Good for chases, mistaken sightings, overheard confessions, and crowd scenes where every witness sees a different angle.

## Secret Geography
Every fifth bridge points toward the Underclock Vault when the moon is behind cloud.

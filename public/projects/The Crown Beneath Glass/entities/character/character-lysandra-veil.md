## Method
Lysandra never lies when a selective truth will do more damage. She turns public grief into policy before anyone has finished mourning.

## Fear
A spontaneous city. A crowd she did not rehearse. A monarch whose power depends on consent instead of paperwork.

## Tell
When she is losing, she stops using names and starts using offices.
